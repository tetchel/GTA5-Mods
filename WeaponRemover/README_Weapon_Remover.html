Better Weapon Remover

What This Mod Does

This mod fixes two related issues I had with vanilla GTA:

  1. You cannot drop weapons.
  2. You must cycle through every possible driveby weapon when you are in a vehicle.
So, this mod allows you to define two lists of weapons: those you don't want in your inventory, and those you don't want to cycle through while driving.
For dropping weapons, you can choose to either drop the listed weapons at the press of a button (default L), or to drop those weapons whenever you pick one up, so that you never have to look at them again!

How to Use This Mod

Download an example unwanted_weapons.txt here, and place it into your GTA 5 dir /scripts/, the same place this mod is installed.

Use a weapon list like this, but without the WEAPON_ prefix.
You must not edit the lines that begin with "~", or add new lines above ~Weapons to drop or avoid:.

You can add weapons to the ~Weapons to drop section to have them dropped either when you press a key, or constantly, depending on the value of DropOnlyOnPress (see 1: below).
You must reload the script (thus applying your weapon list changes) by pressing Insert. Do not do this while on a vehicle or you might lose your weapons!.

You can also add weapons to the ~Weapons to hide when in car: section to have them hidden when you are in a vehicle, so you don't have to cycle through them when selecting a driveby weapon. This list is not affected by any of the settings below.

Changing the Mod Options

Lines 1 and 2 allow you to select the "Mode" of the first part of the mod (see 1:) and the key to drop weapons if you are in DropOnlyOnPress mode (that is, if line 1 is set to true).

1: Change only the value after the = to "true" or "false" (without quotes of course).
If it is set to true, your unwanted weapons will be dropped when you press DropKey (defined in line 2).
Else if it is set to false, you will drop all your unwanted weapons constantly.

2: Set the DropKey, the key that will remove unwanted weapons in DropOnlyOnPress=true mode to any of these that your keyboard supports except for any key marked as a modifier.
